Things to do in Paris

Visit the Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable structures in the world, stands as a testament to architectural innovation and artistic design. Completed in 1889, it was initially constructed as the centerpiece for the 1889 Exposition Universelle, a world's fair held to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution.

Visit the Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum, located in Paris, France, is one of the world's largest and most famous art museums. Originally built as a fortress in the late 12th century, it was transformed into a royal palace and eventually became a public museum in 1793 during the French Revolution. The museum is situated along the right bank of the Seine River and is renowned for its vast collection of art spanning several millennia, from ancient civilizations to the 19th century.
